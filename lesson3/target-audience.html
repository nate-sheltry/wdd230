Our Target Audience

We are targeting local small businesses and businessmen in our area. As well as those interested in changing business policies and businesses relationship in the community.

This includes those seeking to market for their business, starting a new business, or are looking to network with others in the business sphere.

Furthermore we are geared towards those interested in business and changing policies surrounding it, as well as targeting those who are interested in changing and bettering their business relationship within the community.

We also are aiming to make our members aware of educational sources available to them, whether they be a consumer or businessman.

Personas

Persona 1

Persona

Small Business Owner : John Goodman

Demographic

  • Age: 32
  • Education: Bachelor's Degree in Science
  • Experience: Has heard, but is unfamiliar witht he chamber of commerce.
  • Family: Married, 2 young kids

Goals/Reason for Visiting

John runs a local IT and repair company and is looking to grow his business.

Scenarios:

  • John was at his favorite local sandwhich shop when he heard the owner talking about an upcoming meeting at the local chamber of commerce. He asks him about it and the owner shows him the website on his phone so John can learn more in his spare time. John decides to look at the website later after work while on his phone.

  • John is having a hard time getting new customers and wants to find a way he can get other businesses to recommend his services when a customer comes in with a problem they can't deal with that he can fix. Having heard of the chamber of commerce he decides to visit the website and see if they can help him form more relationships with other businesses in the area.

  • One day John is in the middle of making a repair for a customer when he learns he is missing the part he needs, he decides he is going to go to the local Walmart. Later John wonders whether there is another solution he could find so that he can keep a regular stock of the eletronic parts he needs. He decides to look on the chamber of commerce to see if there are any members that he could partner with for parts to help both their businesses.

  • When John's son comes home form school he hears about how the school partnered with a local business, Smart Greens, to help get students interested in agriculture and nutrition. Everyone loved their visit and the students want them to come back again. John decides he thinks reaching out to his local district would help parents and kids alike hear about his company and increase business and standing in his community. As such he goes to his local area's chamber of commerce site to see if they can help him facilitate such a collaberation.

  • John is struggling to get the word out about his new company and feels he is lacking in his marketing department. He decides he needs help and doesn't know where to start, he searched for a few marketing firms, but isn't sure if they are the right fit for him. Thats when he decides he is going to take a look at the chamber of commerce website for his area to see who they recommend for marketing and who are trusted by other members.

Persona 2

Persona

Business Counsultant : Robert Fisher

Demographic

  • Age: 28
  • Education: Bachelor's in Business
  • Experience: Knows of the Chamber of Commerce and is familiar.
  • Family: Single, no Children

Goals/Reason for Visiting

Robert is hoping to help his clients take advantage of the benefits available to him and keep them aware of ongoing changes in their area.

Scenarios:

  • Robert in preperation for his next client goes to his local chamber of commerce's site and finds out which applicable businesses are offering discounts to help his client.

  • Robert is helping a startup when his client tells him he wants to have a solid place in the community but can't currently afford to do anything. Robert decides to look on the chamber of commerce site to see what kind of fundraising opportunities he can help his client take advantage of so they can organize something to give back to their community.

  • Robert has a new client who has been running their small business successfully for a few years and has never joined the chamber of commerce. His client seems hesitant to join as a member due to the member fees and thinks the fees outway the benefits. Robert decides that he is going to make a list of all the benefits offered by the chamber of commerce in their local area and the upcoming events that could benefit his client.

  • Robert hears of a new local policy their county is trying to implement via an ad on tv. It sounds like it would be terrible for businesses but everything online he can find about it seems to be split 50/50. His client asks him about it, but Robert isn't sure what the policy does. On his lunchbreak he decides he is going to use his phone see what his local chamber of commerce has for resources to learn more about it.

  • A client is telling Robert that he feels like he is at odds with the businesses near him due to an issue with parking. Robert looks up when the next meeting for their local chamber of commerce is and sees if the other business owners are going to be attending so that he could help his client meet with them and discuss possible solutions to their problem.

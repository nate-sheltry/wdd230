Historical Information

Starting in Carlsbad, California, we at Bountiful Foods have made bringing fresh and healthy food options to people our main goal. Whether it be for the local Californian or a visiting tourist, we make eating organic easy and convenient.

While Carlsbad at one point in time was known for its mineral water spas and health resorts, what some might not know is how said mineral water plays a vital role in helping the local agriculture and is one of the many reasons Carlsbad was known for its fertile land!
Despite its current fame being centered on its recreational activities, we aim to honor the health-based reputation Carlsbad has always had.

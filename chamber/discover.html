Welome to Little Creek

Welcome to the town of Little Creek! A small town in Delaware near the Bayshore Byway.

Enjoy Access to the Water

Visit Little Landing, Wildlife Haven. With access to the water, enjoy the various water based activities, such as boating and fishing, while enjoying the wilfdlife.

Experience the Rich History

Little Creek was first inhabited by pirates, before becoming a very prosperous town in the 1800's due to the oyster industry thriving. As such, Little Creek has a rich history from its foundings which can be seen in the town's architecture.

Relax at Pickering Beach

At Pickering beach, you will be witness to the hundreds and thousands of horshoe crab that arrive during the spring into early summer. Bird watchers will be able to view the shore birds, as they feed off the horshoe crabs eggs.

Observe Birds at the Main Tract

At the Main Tract you will be able to see various waterbirds and waterfowl from the observation tower, leading to great views and wonderful sights.

Visit Old Stone Tavern

A historic name located in Little Creek, this home is a gregorian-style house with a kitchen wing attached. The original owners owned a tavern, but the name of the house itself is a mystery, as it was never a tavern.
